1. Summary

is a fully offline, single-user app. No data collected It collects, stores, and transmits no personal information of any kind.

2. Personal information collected

None. The app does not collect any personal information — no name, email, phone number, location, advertising ID (AAID), or other identifiers. There is no sign-up, login, or account system.

3. Data stored on your device

The following items are stored only on your device (via SharedPreferences) so the app can function:

  • Gameplay-related statistics (correct/wrong counts, streaks, best records, etc.)
  • App preferences (e.g. language, difficulty toggle)

This data is never transmitted to any external server and is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app or clear its data from your device settings.

4. Permissions

The app requests no Android permissions. It does not even use the internet permission.

5. Third-party sharing

None. No data is transmitted to or shared with any third party.

6. Advertising & analytics SDKs

None. No ad networks, analytics, or crash-reporting SDKs are included in the app.

7. Children's privacy

The app does not collect personal information from any user, including children.

8. Your rights

You can erase all locally stored data at any time by:

  • Using the in-app stats RESET action (where available)
  • OS Settings → Apps → Storage → Clear data
  • Uninstalling the app

9. Changes

If this policy changes, the document will be updated alongside the next app release; the effective date above will reflect the change.
